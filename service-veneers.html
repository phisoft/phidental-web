Veneers
Porcelain dental veneers are thin, porcelain shells bonded to the front of your teeth. They are primarily used to improve the shape, lighten colour, close spaces and in some cases, to align the teeth.
Veneers is also the treatment of choice in discolourations that are too dark to respond well to teeth whitening. These long lasting, ultra-thin porcelain shells can change the colour and the shape of the natural teeth and are an effective way to transform your smile.


Benefits of Dental Veneers
Desire a more youthful smile? Porcelain dental veneers can provide excellent aesthetic results to improve your smile:
  • Significantly change the colour of your teeth (if teeth whitening cannot lighten them, or root canal treated teeth that are discoloured)
  • Improve the shape and even align misaligned teeth.
  • Close ugly spaces in the front teeth.
  • Veneers are more resistant to staining from coffee/tea/red wine, than normal fillings, as they are made of high-tech materials.
Our laboratory will customise all the veneers to achieve the most aesthetic and pleasing results. Bid goodbye to your worn-out, discoloured and tired smile. Rejuvenating you and your self-esteem!
Veneering Procedures
  1. Consultation appointment (Approximately 30 mins)
    Detailed examination and analysis of your smile and needs.
  2. First appointment (Approximately 1 hour)
    Prepare the teeth and take impressions for the veneer. Our dentists will work with our laboratory to fabricate the dental veneers for you.
  3. Second appointment (Approximately 1 hour)
    Seating of completed veneers and permanently bonding them to the teeth.
  4. Review of completed veneers.
    Do note that in some cases when patients have parafunctional habits like night-grinding (Bruxism) or clenching, night-guards may be prescribed for the protection of these veneers.
